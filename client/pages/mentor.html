Dr. Kyla McMullen

Computer Science Professor

My mentor is Professor McMullen who teaches in the College of Engineering at the University of Florida. She specializes in 3D Audio Processing, Multi-model Interfaces, Psycho-acoustics and Virtual Environments. In the Sound Pab Lab she seeks to use 3D audio to create assistive technology, and to discover patterns in big data. The Sound Pad Lab also researches perceptual requirements necessary to render realistic spatial sound using headphones in virtual environments.
